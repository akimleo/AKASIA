FAQ



What does AKASIA mean?

Angkatan Anak Seni (AKASIA) is a combination of cultural clubs.

What is the top genre in AKASIA?

There are 2 top genre which is Modern and Traditional. Mostly there are Traditional dancers than Modern dancers but that doesn't mean we focused on just 1 genre, we can balanced it suitable to our community.

Where are the usual practice venue?

Usually we will held practice at Student Square Level 2 or in Studio nearby such as Shamelin Star Studio at Level 7.

What if we have never danced before?

If you have never dance before, we will teach how to dance to you, give you a few tips and place you in a competition suitable to your genre.
